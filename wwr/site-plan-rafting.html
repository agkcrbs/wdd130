Overview

Purpose

This website will introduce visitors to a (fictional) company that organizes rafting excursions.

Its goals are to show the rafting experience, encourage visitors to purchase a trip, and provide contact information.

Audience

The audience is outing planners and heads of families. Price, safety, and interest will factor into their decisions.

Users of computers and other devices will access the site, using tabs/links to navigate between pages.
